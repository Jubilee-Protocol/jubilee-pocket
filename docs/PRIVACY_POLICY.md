# Privacy Policy

**Jubilee Pocket**  
**Effective Date**: February 2026  
**Last Updated**: February 2026

## Overview

Jubilee Pocket ("we", "our", or "the App") is a mobile application developed by Jubilee Labs LLC that enables users to interact with the Solana blockchain. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Wallet Address**: Your public Solana wallet address when you connect via Mobile Wallet Adapter
- **Transaction Data**: Records of on-chain transactions you initiate through the App

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- Private keys (never leaves your device/Seed Vault)
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
- Location data
- Device identifiers for tracking purposes

## How We Use Information

We use the information solely to:
- Display your wallet balance and transaction history
- Execute blockchain transactions you authorize
- Provide push notifications about your vault status (LTV warnings, harvest completion)

## Data Storage

- **On-Chain Data**: All transaction data is stored on the public Solana blockchain
- **Local Storage**: App preferences are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage
- **No Server Storage**: We do not operate servers that store user data

## Third-Party Services

The App integrates with:
- **Solana Network**: For blockchain transactions
- **Pyth Network**: For price oracle data
- **Jupiter Aggregator**: For token swap quotes

These services have their own privacy policies.

## Security

- All wallet interactions use the Mobile Wallet Adapter protocol
- Private keys are secured by the Solana Seeker's Seed Vault hardware
- No sensitive data is transmitted to external servers

## Children's Privacy

Jubilee Pocket is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted in the dapp and this document.
